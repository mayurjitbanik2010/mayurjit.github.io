Bulk Add Questions

Paste multiple questions at once. One question per block, separated by a blank line. Each block needs: the question, then 4 lines starting with A) B) C) D), then a line starting with Answer: (A/B/C/D), and optionally Marks: and Negative: (defaults to 4 and -1 if omitted).

Upload Questions from PDF

Upload a PDF of your question paper. Works best when questions are numbered (e.g. 1. or Q1.) and options are labelled A/B/C/D (as A), (A), or A.). If the PDF includes an answer key (e.g. Answer: C under each question, or a list like 1-C 2-A 3-B), correct answers are detected automatically — otherwise you'll pick them in the review step.

Review the detected questions below. Fix anything that looks wrong, set the correct answer where missing, then add them to the set.
